Study Tips 📚
01
⏱️
The Pomodoro Method
Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 rounds, take a 20-minute break. This keeps your brain fresh and focused without burning out.
02
📵
Kill the Distractions
Put your phone face-down or in another room. Use apps like Forest or Focus Mode. A distraction-free environment = 3x more productive study time.
03
🗓️
Plan Your Week on Sunday
Every Sunday, write down your top 3 goals for the week. Break big tasks into small daily actions. Clarity the night before = calm and focused mornings.
04
✏️
Write, Don't Just Read
Active recall beats passive reading every time. Write notes in your own words, make flashcards, or teach the concept out loud. Your brain retains what it creates.
05
💧
Hydrate & Move
Dehydration kills focus. Keep a water bottle at your desk. Every hour, stand up, stretch for 2 minutes — your concentration will thank you.
06
🌙
Sleep is Non-Negotiable
Your brain consolidates memories during sleep. Pulling all-nighters backfires. Aim for 7–8 hours and do a quick review before bed for maximum retention.
